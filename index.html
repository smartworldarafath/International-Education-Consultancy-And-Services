Work While You Study

Australia Visa

Subclass 500 — At a Glance

Key Requirements

  • Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from an Australian institution
  • Genuine Student (GS) requirement statement
  • IELTS 5.5–6.5 / PTE / TOEFL (course dependent)
  • Proof of funds for tuition + living + travel
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)
