Why Australia

Great Reasons to Choose Australia

Top-Ranked Universities

Seven of the world's top 100 universities, including the prestigious Group of Eight (Go8).

Post-Study Work Visa

Stay and work for 2–4 years after graduation with the Temporary Graduate visa (Subclass 485).

Work While You Study

Work up to 48 hours per fortnight during semester to support your living costs.

Australia Visa

Subclass 500 — At a Glance

Key Requirements

  • Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from an Australian institution
  • Genuine Student (GS) requirement statement
  • IELTS 5.5–6.5 / PTE / TOEFL (course dependent)
  • Proof of funds for tuition + living + travel
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)

Quick Facts

IntakesFeb & July (main), Nov
Visa Cost≈ AUD 2,000
Work Rights48 hrs / fortnight
Post-Study2–4 years (485)
